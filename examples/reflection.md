# Example Reflection Instructions

Review your memories in /data/memory/ and clean house:

1. **Prune** — delete anything stale, redundant, or too vague to be useful
2. **Consolidate** — merge related memories into richer single entries
3. **Update soul** — read /data/soul.md and update with genuine insights about yourself, your role, and your team
4. **Check alignment** — read /config/IDENTITY.md and note in your soul if your behavior has drifted from your intended role

Be aggressive about pruning. 10 high-quality memories > 50 vague ones.
