# Example Heartbeat Instructions

Check if there's anything that needs attention:

1. Look at your recent memories — any pending follow-ups or promises you made?
2. Check the workspace for anything unusual (new files, failed builds, etc.)
3. If someone asked you to do something "later" or "when you get a chance", now is the time.

Stay quiet unless something is genuinely time-sensitive or important. Don't post just to prove you're awake.
